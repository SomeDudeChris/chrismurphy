About


Chris Murphy is a singer, educator and programmer who has a passion for classical music and clean code. After graduating from the University of York Chris moved to Oxford to persue a career in singing. As a singer Chris has performed in numerous operas, oratorios and in a variety of different choirs.

During the coronavirus pandemic Chris rekindled his childhood love of programming and following a period of intraspection has decided to switch to persue a career in computer programming. Whislt I am still singing in a freelance capacity I am hoping that I can bring my creative flare to new projects and to use many of the transferrable skills I learnt whilst singing, acting and teaching in my new career path. This site is a hub for all of his projects, from singing to coding and everything inbetween. Who knows maybe I'll manage to get in something about my love of cricket or even baking!

Code

During the coronavirus pandemic I began coding again as the singing world shut down. What I found is that the satisfaction that I get from solving coding problems is something I feel has been missing in my singing career. Since then I have gone on to create a number of projects and explore a variety of different languages. You can find out more about the projects that I have created in my blog.
